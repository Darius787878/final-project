the Hand of Noxus

Darius

Darius is one of the most feared and renowned warriors of Noxus, an empire known for its militaristic and expansionist nature. As the "Hand of Noxus," Darius holds a position of immense power and influence, both on and off the battlefield. His story is one of raw strength, brutal efficiency, and unwavering loyalty to his nation. Darius was born into the lower echelons of Noxian society. His early life was marked by hardship and struggle, which forged him into a relentless and formidable warrior. His rise to prominence came through his sheer prowess in combat and his ability to rally and lead his troops with both fear and respect.
